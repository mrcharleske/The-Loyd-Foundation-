Introduction

The Loyd Foundation ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (the "Site"), interact with our services, or donate. We comply with applicable laws, including GDPR for EU users and Kenyan Data Protection Act.

What Data We Collect

We collect minimal personal data:

  • Donation/Contact Forms: Name, email, phone, message (via Formspree).
  • Newsletter: Email only.
  • Analytics: IP address, browser type, pages visited (via Google Analytics if enabled—opt-out available).
  • PWA/Offline: Local storage for queued forms (cleared on sync).

We do not collect sensitive data (e.g., health, financial beyond donations).

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used to:

  • Process donations and send receipts.
  • Respond to inquiries and newsletters.
  • Improve Site (e.g., analytics for user experience).
  • Comply with legal obligations (e.g., tax records).

Data is retained for 5 years for donations, 2 years for contacts, or as required by law.

Sharing Your Data

We do not sell your data. We share only with:

  • Service providers (e.g., Formspree for forms, Stripe for payments—future).
  • Law enforcement if required.
  • Partners for program delivery (anonymized).

International transfers (e.g., US-Kenya) use standard contractual clauses.

Your Rights

Under GDPR/KDPA, you have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your data.
  • Object to processing or withdraw consent.
  • Portability (e.g., export your data).

Exercise rights by emailing loydfoundation.co@gmail.com. We respond within 30 days.

Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies for Site function (e.g., session). Optional analytics cookies—decline via browser settings. No third-party trackers without consent.

For PWA, localStorage stores temporary offline data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with date. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

Contact Us

For privacy questions: loydfoundation.co@gmail.com. Data Protection Officer: dpo@loydfoundation.co.
