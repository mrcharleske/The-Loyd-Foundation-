Our Programs

Transformative initiatives designed to uplift communities and build a better tomorrow.

Feeding Programs

Hunger is one of the greatest barriers to opportunity. The Loyd Foundation believes no child or family should go to bed hungry. Through our Feeding Programs, we deliver nutritious meals to vulnerable children, families, and communities across Kenya and beyond.

These programs not only provide immediate relief but also foster dignity, hope, and a stronger foundation for education and productivity. By partnering with schools, local organizations, and farmers, we ensure sustainability and empower communities to break the cycle of hunger.

Youth Empowerment

Africa’s greatest wealth lies in its youth. The Loyd Foundation invests in empowering the next generation through mentorship, entrepreneurship training, leadership development, and access to technology.

We believe every young person deserves the chance to dream, innovate, and create impact. From vocational training to startup incubation, our programs equip young people with the skills, confidence, and networks to thrive in today’s global economy.

Education Projects

Education is the gateway to equality and progress. The Loyd Foundation champions access to quality education for children in underserved communities by providing scholarships, learning materials, digital literacy programs, and school infrastructure support.

We are committed to ensuring that every child, regardless of background, has the opportunity to learn and achieve their potential. Education is not just about literacy — it’s about unlocking creativity, nurturing leaders, and preparing future generations for a better tomorrow.

Farmers & SMEs Empowerment

Smallholder farmers and small businesses form the backbone of Africa’s economy. Yet, they face challenges such as limited access to markets, financing, and modern technologies.

The Loyd Foundation empowers farmers and SMEs by offering training in modern farming methods, linking them with markets, and providing financial literacy programs.

Our vision is to transform farmers into agripreneurs and SMEs into engines of growth that drive prosperity, reduce poverty, and create lasting opportunities across communities.
