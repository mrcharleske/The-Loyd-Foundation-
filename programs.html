Our Programs

Targeted initiatives providing tools, training, and resources—from market access for farmers to scholarships for students.

SME Empowerment

Training, mentorship, and micro-grants to help local businesses scale responsibly, focusing on bookkeeping, digital presence, and market-readiness.

Farmers Empowerment

Capacity-building, quality inputs, and buyer linkages, promoting climate-smart methods and cooperative marketing to raise incomes.

Feeding Program

School and community feeding programs delivering nutritious meals to children, reducing absenteeism, and sourcing food locally.

Education & Scholarships

Scholarships for tuition, supplies, and mentorship for high-potential students, with progress tracking and guidance.
