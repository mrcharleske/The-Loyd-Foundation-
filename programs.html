Our Programs

Targeted initiatives providing tools, training, and resources—from market access for farmers to scholarships for students.

Click any program to support it directly.

SME Empowerment

Training, mentorship, and micro-grants to help local businesses scale responsibly, focusing on bookkeeping, digital presence, and market-readiness. Helped 50+ businesses double revenue last year.

Farmers Empowerment

Capacity-building, quality inputs, and buyer linkages, promoting climate-smart methods and cooperative marketing to raise incomes. Supported 1,200 farmers with 30% yield increase.

Feeding Program

School and community feeding programs delivering nutritious meals to children, reducing absenteeism, and sourcing food locally. Served 50,000+ meals, improving attendance by 25%.

Education & Scholarships

Scholarships for tuition, supplies, and mentorship for high-potential students, with progress tracking and guidance. Awarded 45 scholarships, with 90% graduation rate.
