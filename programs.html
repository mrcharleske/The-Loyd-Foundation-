Our Programs

Discover the transformative initiatives uplifting communities and shaping a better tomorrow!

Feeding Programs

Hunger is a barrier we’re determined to break! The Loyd Foundation’s Feeding Programs deliver nutritious meals to vulnerable children, families, and communities across Kenya and beyond. Imagine the smiles of a child nourished for the first time, or a family gaining the strength to pursue their dreams— that’s the impact we create. By partnering with local schools, farmers, and organizations, we ensure sustainability, turning relief into a foundation for education and productivity.

Youth Empowerment

Africa’s future shines brightest in its youth, and we’re here to light the way! The Loyd Foundation invests in the next generation with mentorship, entrepreneurship training, leadership workshops, and access to cutting-edge technology. Every young person deserves to dream big—our programs turn those dreams into action, from vocational skills to startup incubators, building a network of confident, innovative leaders ready to shape the world.

Education Projects

Education is the key to unlocking a world of possibilities! The Loyd Foundation champions access to quality learning for children in underserved communities with scholarships, learning materials, digital literacy programs, and infrastructure support. Every classroom we build, every book we provide, and every child we teach is a step toward equality and progress—nurturing creativity and preparing future leaders for a brighter tomorrow.

Farmers & SMEs Empowerment

Smallholder farmers and small businesses are the heartbeat of Africa’s economy! The Loyd Foundation empowers them by providing training in modern farming techniques, linking them to global markets, and offering financial literacy programs. Our vision is to transform farmers into thriving agripreneurs and SMEs into engines of growth, reducing poverty and creating a ripple effect of prosperity across communities—your support can make this vision real!
