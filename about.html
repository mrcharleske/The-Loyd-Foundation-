About Nathan Loyd

Nathan Loyd is a trailblazing entrepreneur, CEO, and author whose remarkable 30-year career has left an indelible mark on international trade, real estate, agriculture, and mineral processing. His journey is a testament to innovation, sustainability, and a deep commitment to empowering communities across Africa and beyond. With a heart full of compassion, Nathan has turned his successes into a powerful force for good through The Loyd Foundation.

As the visionary behind Avoveg Health LLC and Avoveg Health Kenya Ltd, Nathan revolutionized Kenya’s avocado industry, establishing it as a leading exporter to Europe and the Middle East. His leadership created thousands of opportunities for local farmers, strengthened trade relationships, and showcased the power of sustainable agriculture.

At DN International in Rwanda, Nathan spearheaded transformative real estate projects, building large-scale housing and commercial developments that fueled urban growth and improved living standards. His strategic vision turned challenges into opportunities, leaving a legacy of progress.

Today, through The Loyd Foundation, Nathan channels his expertise and resources into life-changing philanthropic efforts. From pioneering education and youth empowerment programs to supporting farmers and SMEs, and advancing community healthcare and environmental sustainability, his work inspires hope. His vision? To give back to the community and ignite the next generation of leaders, entrepreneurs, and changemakers across Africa.
