About Nathan Loyd

Nathan Loyd is a seasoned entrepreneur, CEO, and author with over 30 years of transformative experience in international trade, real estate, agriculture, and mineral processing. His career has been marked by a commitment to innovation, sustainability, and empowering communities across Africa and beyond.

As the Founder & CEO of Avoveg Health LLC and Avoveg Health Kenya Ltd, Nathan was instrumental in establishing Kenya as a leading exporter of avocados to Europe and the Middle East, creating opportunities for farmers and strengthening international trade relationships.

He also led DN International, a major real estate firm in Rwanda, where he spearheaded large-scale housing and commercial projects that contributed significantly to Rwanda’s urban development.

Today, through The Loyd Foundation, Nathan dedicates his expertise and resources to philanthropic initiatives — from education and youth empowerment programs, to supporting farmers and SMEs, and advancing community healthcare and environmental sustainability.

His vision is rooted in giving back to the community and inspiring the next generation of leaders, entrepreneurs, and changemakers across Africa.
