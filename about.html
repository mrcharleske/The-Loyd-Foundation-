About The Loyd Foundation

Founded in 2020, we empower communities through targeted programs in Kenya and beyond.

"Empowering one life at a time builds resilient futures for generations." — Loyd Foundation Team
Our Programs

Our Mission

We bridge opportunities in entrepreneurship, agriculture, nutrition, and education for sustainable change. By partnering with local leaders and leveraging innovative tools, we create pathways to dignity and self-reliance.

Our vision: A world where every community thrives through empowered individuals.

Key Projects

Highlighting our flagship efforts that drive real-world impact.

Community Leadership Training

Empowering local leaders in Kisumu with skills in advocacy and resource management. Reached 200 participants, leading to 3 new cooperatives formed.

Sustainable Farms Initiative

Innovative agriculture pilot in Nakuru, introducing drought-resistant seeds to 500 smallholder farmers, boosting yields by 40% despite climate challenges.

Scholarship Expansion Outreach

Extending scholarships to rural schools in Rift Valley, providing mentorship and supplies to 100 high-potential students, with 85% advancing to higher education.
