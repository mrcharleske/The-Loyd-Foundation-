About The Loyd Foundation

Empowering Communities. Transforming Lives. Building Legacies.

Nathan Loyd – Founder & Visionary

Nathan Loyd is a seasoned entrepreneur, CEO, and author with over three decades of experience in international trade, real estate, agriculture, and mineral processing. As the Founder and CEO of Avoveg Health LLC and Avoveg Health Kenya Ltd, he played a pivotal role in positioning Kenya as a leading exporter of avocados to Europe and the Middle East.

His leadership extended beyond agriculture, where he steered DN International, one of Rwanda’s major real estate firms, fostering regional growth and sustainable development. His global business acumen, combined with his passion for social transformation, inspired the creation of The Loyd Foundation — a platform dedicated to giving back and shaping future generations.

Our Story & Purpose

The Loyd Foundation was born out of a simple belief: success is not complete until it uplifts others. After decades of pioneering business ventures and international achievements, Nathan Loyd established the foundation to create opportunities for communities across Africa.

The foundation focuses on four pillars: Feeding Programs, Youth Empowerment, Education Access, and Farmers & SMEs Empowerment. These pillars represent the heartbeat of our mission — to strengthen the social and economic fabric of our society.

By building partnerships, promoting sustainability, and fostering innovation, The Loyd Foundation aspires to transform lives and ensure that future generations inherit a world of opportunity, dignity, and hope.
